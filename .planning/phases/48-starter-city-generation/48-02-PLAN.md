    **Step 1: Analyze each screenshot with zai.**

    For EACH screenshot in C:/tmp/vb_visual_test/48_terrain_*.png, call zai `analyze_image` with this prompt:

    ```
    Rate this Blender-generated dark fantasy game terrain on a scale of PLACEHOLDER / BASIC / DECENT / GOOD / AAA.

    Check these specific qualities:
    1. TERRAIN SHAPE: Does it have natural-looking hills, valleys, and elevation? Or is it flat/repetitive?
    2. MATERIALS: Are materials applied (earth tones, grass, rock)? Or is it default grey?
    3. EROSION: Are there visible erosion channels, gullies, natural weathering? Or is it smooth?
    4. RIVER (if visible): Do river banks have gentle 5-15 degree slopes? Or are they staircase grid steps?
    5. WATER (if visible): Is the water a shaped river mesh following the terrain? Or a single flat plane flooding everything?
    6. ROADS (if visible): Do roads follow the terrain surface? Or are they floating/underground?
    7. DARK FANTASY PALETTE: Desaturated greens, brown earth, grey stone, muted colors? Or bright/saturated/default?
    8. CLIFF/ELEVATION: Are there visible cliff faces with scree at the base? Or smooth slopes everywhere?

    Give an overall grade and list specific issues to fix.
    ```

    **Step 2: Act on zai feedback.**
    - If grade is GOOD or AAA on ALL screenshots: Proceed. Terrain is ready.
    - If grade is DECENT on ANY: Adjust parameters and regenerate the specific problematic area.
    - If grade is BASIC or PLACEHOLDER on ANY: This is a BLOCKER. The prior phase wiring is likely broken.

    **Fix-regenerate cycle (if needed):**
    - Default grey materials -> try `blender_environment(action="paint_biome", biome="thornwood_forest")`
    - Flat water plane -> try `blender_environment(action="create_water", rivers=[...])` with explicit river spec
    - Staircase river banks -> increase erosion_iterations to 12000, regenerate terrain
    - No cliff features -> increase height_scale to 30.0 or add cliff terrain features manually
    - After each fix, re-screenshot and re-analyze with zai
